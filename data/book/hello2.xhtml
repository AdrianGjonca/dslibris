Hello in many languages

There isn't an equivalent for this word in all languages, but many have something similar. In some languages, different words for hello are used when answering the phone.

Guide to abbreviations: inf = informal, frm = formal, sg = singular (said to one person), dl = dual (said to two people), pl = plural (said to three or more people), rsp = rsp
