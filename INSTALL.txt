INSTALLATION

1. DLDI patch dslibris.nds to work for your media. eg. for R4DS

	dlditool r4tf.dldi dslibris.nds

2. copy all files from the distribution .zip to the root directory
of your DS media.

3. Put any XHTML you have on the root of your media.

4. run dslibris.nds on the DS.

For tips and help, see http://rhaleblian.wordpress.com
